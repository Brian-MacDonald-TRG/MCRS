	<maintsk>
		<replace>
			<proc>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<caution>
							<trim.para>The individual drawers weigh 150 lbs. You should have a suitable stand or work bench in place before removing the drawer. Failure to comply may result in injury or death to personnel.</trim.para>
						</caution>
						<note>
							<trim.para>There are sixteen drawers on the TDRT. This procedure covers the replacement of bolts or the drawer of one section. The procedure is the same for all of the drawers needing repair.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Original drawer bolts have a shoulder that requires the drawer to be removed so that the bolt itself can be removed from the top. Replacement bolts no longer have the shoulder and can be threaded into and removed from the TDRT through the bottom. This allows for future installation or removal/replacement of individual drawer bolts without the need for removing the entire drawer assembly.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Lubricate the drawer bolts with penetrating oil to facilitate loosening.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Loosen the six bolts <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0001" label="1"/>) that secure the drawer assembly.</para>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0001">
					<title>Pull Out Drawer Bolts</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000481"/>
					</figure>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<caution>
							<trim.para>Once the stop key bolts are removed the drawer assembly is free and could accidently fall from the TDRT if attention is not paid to its positioning. Failure to use caution could result in serious injury to personnel and or damage to the equipment.</trim.para>
						</caution>
						<para>Loosen and remove the 6 (six) bolts that hold the drawer stop keys.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0002">
					<title>Stop Key Bolt Removal</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000482"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<para>With a stand or table in place, remove the drawer assembly <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0003" label="1"/>) from the TDRT Deck.</para>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0003">
					<title>Drawer Assembly Removed</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000483"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>If the drawer bolt is frozen or cracked at the bottom, it may be necessary to weld a nut to the top of the bolt to facilitate removal.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Remove the damaged drawer bolt <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0004" label="1"/>. It may be easier to feed bolt up through the top.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0004">
					<title>Drawer Bolt Removal</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000484"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<para>Once the old bolt is removed, make sure the threads in the bolt hole on the TDRT are clean.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Install the new Drawer Bolt.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Place the Stop Keys in the TDRT Deck, at the edge of the opening for the Drawer Assembly.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Using one of the Stop Key Bolts for each key, temporarily hold each key in place by threading the bolt up through the drain hole <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0005" label="1"/> of the TDRT and into the center hole of the key.</para>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0005">
					<title>Drain Hole</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000485"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<para>Using an assistant, insert the Drawer Assembly <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0006" label="1"/> into the TDRT Deck. Place it in the "Out" position.</para>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0006">
					<title>Drawer Assembly Installation</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000486"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<para>Line up and loosely insert the 2 remaining Stop Key Bolts for each Stop Key.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove the 3rd Stop Key Bolt from itsï¿½ temporary location through the drain hole and insert it in its proper location for the Stop Key.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Tighten the 6 Stop Key Bolts <callout assocfig="M00098-9-2320-451-F0007" label="1"/>.</para>
				</step1>

					<figure id="M00098-9-2320-451-F0007">
					<title>Stop Key Bolts</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000487"/>
					</figure>

				<step1>
					<para>Position the Drawer Assembly in the desired position (In or out) and tighten the Drawer Bolts to 80 ft.-lbs.</para>
				</step1>
			</proc>
		</replace>
