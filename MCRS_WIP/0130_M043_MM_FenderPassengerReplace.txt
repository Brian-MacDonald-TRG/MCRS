<maintsk>
	<install>
			<proc>
				<step1>
					<para>Install serviceable fender brackets if needed.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Obtain a serviceable fender</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Using an assistant position serviceable fender under fender brackets.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Clamp and brace fender flush to fender brackets using clamps and or supports.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Using a 5/16" drill bit; drill all accessible bolt holes through fender using fender brackets as a guide.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Using an assistant, reposition drilled fender and clamp at the front and rear fender brackets only.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Loosely install all hardware until all bolts, nuts, washers, and lock washers have been installed. Perform final tightening after installation of components is complete.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Clamps and supports may be repositioned on fender as required during installation of hardware.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Loosely install serviceable bolts, washers, lock washers, and nuts through front fender bracket, and serviceable fender.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove clamps at rear fender bracket.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reposition mud flap.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Install serviceable bolts, washers, lock washers and nuts through rear bracket, mud flap, and serviceable fender.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reinstall tool box IAW <xref wpid="M00045-9-2320-451"/></para>
				</step1>
			</proc>
		</install>
