							<trim.para>High pressure is present in the 250M Fifth Wheel Towing Device hydraulic system. Do not disconnect any hydraulic system component unless hydraulic system pressure has been relieved. Ensure each hydraulic control lever is moved several times through all positions and hydraulic tank relief valve is slowly loosened to relieve pressure. Failure to comply may result in severe injury or death to personnel.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Spilled hydraulic oil is very slippery. Be careful when entering or working in area. Wipe up any spilled oil immediately. Failure to comply may result in severe injury or death to personnel.</trim.para>
						</warning>
						<caution>
							<trim.para>Always clean around fittings before disconnecting or connecting hoses or fittings. Ensure area is clean before installing hydraulic components. Failure to comply may result in damage to equipment.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Cover, cap, or plug all openings, ports, and tube or hose ends when they are disconnected. Failure to comply may result in damage to equipment. Fittings must be installed and hand-tightened. If a fitting cannot be hand tightened, it may be cross-threaded or have damaged threads. Use wrench only for final tightening. Failure to comply may result in damage to equipment.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Do not use Teflon tape as a sealer on any fittings. It can separate from the fittings and cause control valves, relief valves, and actuators to become contaminated and fail. Do not apply sealant to the first threads of NPT fittings. If sealant enters the hydraulic system, it may cause components to fail.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>When connecting a hose to a fitting, always use two wrenches. Use one wrench to turn the swivel nut onto the fitting, and use another wrench to keep the hose from rotating. If the hose rotates, it can loosen the other end of the hose or loosen the fitting at the other end.</trim.para>
						</caution>
						<note>
