<title>TOOL IDENTIFICATION LIST INTRODUCTION</title>
<subpara1>
<title>Scope</title>
<para>This work package lists all special
tools and equipment needed to maintain the
Modular Catastrophic Recovery System (MCRS).</para>
</subpara1><subpara1>
<title>Explanation of Columns in the Tool
Identification List</title>
<para><emphasis emph="bold">Column (1) Item
No.</emphasis> This number is assigned to
the entry in the list and is referenced in
the initial setup to identify the item (e.g.,
Safety Helmet (WP 0056, Item 4)).</para>
<para><emphasis emph="bold">Column (2) Item
Name.</emphasis> This column lists the item
name or identification of the tool or test
equipment (e.g., Mallet, Rawhide).</para>
<para><emphasis emph="bold">Column (3) National
Stock Number (NSN).</emphasis> This is the
National Stock Number (NSN) of the tool or
test equipment.</para>
<para><emphasis emph="bold">Column (4) Part
Number/(CAGEC).</emphasis> Part Number/(CAGEC).
Indicates the primary number used by the manufacturer
of the item to identify the item. The manufacturers
Commercial and Government Entity Code (CAGEC)
is also included.</para>
<para><emphasis emph="bold">Column (5) Reference.</emphasis
> This column identifies the authorizing supply
catalog or RPSTL for items listed in this
work package.</para>
</subpara1></para0></intro><toolidlist>
