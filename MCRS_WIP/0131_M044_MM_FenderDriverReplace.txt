		<install>
			<proc>
				<title>INSTALL</title>
				<step1>
					<para>Install serviceable fender brackets if needed.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Obtain a serviceable fender</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Be sure to route electric control box wiring as noted when positioning serviceable fender.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Using an assistant position serviceable fender under fender brackets.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Clamp and brace fender flush to fender brackets using clamps and or supports <callout assocfig="M00044-9-2320-451-F0014"/>.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="M00044-9-2320-451-F0014">
					<title>Fender clamped for drilling, and close up view of electric control box wire routing at 3rd fender bracket</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000422"></graphic>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>It may be necessary to mark the rear bolt hole locations for each of the fender brackets due to interference with the wet line supply lines. Remove the fender and drill these holes after drilling all accessible bolt hole locations first.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Using a 5/16 inch drill bit; drill all accessible bolt holes through fender using fender brackets as a guide.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove fender and drill any hole locations that were inaccessible and marked.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Using an assistant, reposition drilled fender and clamp at the front and rear fender brackets only.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove wire ties from 12v/24v junction box and electric control box. Reposition on fender. Be sure to re-route wiring as removed.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Loosely install all hardware until all bolts, nuts, washers, and lock washers have been installed. Perform final tightening after installation of components is complete.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Reposition the front and rear control box step cover brackets on the fender brackets</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reposition air brake assembly on fender brackets</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Clamps and supports may be repositioned on fender as required during installation of hardware.</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Starting at the front, and through the third fender bracket, loosely install serviceable bolts, washers, lock washers, and nuts through all component brackets, fender brackets, and serviceable fender</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Remove clamps at rear fender bracket</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reposition mud flap, rear gladhand bracket, and work light</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Install serviceable bolts, washers, lock washers and nuts through rear component brackets, fender bracket and serviceable fender</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Install 4 each nuts and lock washers on the bottom of the 12V/24V junction box and the electric control box</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<note>
							<trim.para>Watch that air lines and electric cables are not pinched when tightening hardware</trim.para>
						</note>
						<para>Tighten all hardware for the fender, 12V/24V junction box, and electric control box</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reinstall placard using serviceable screws</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reposition winch diverter assembly</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Install 3 serviceable bolts to secure winch diverter bracket to fender bracket.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reconnect ground wires for the 12V/24V junction box and the electric control box at the bottom bolt holes of the second and third fender bracket using one each serviceable bolt and lock washer.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Reconnect batteries</para>
				</step1>
			</proc>
		</install>
