		<align>
			<proc>
				<title>ALIGN</title>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<warning>
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Object">
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-foot">
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-hand">
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-wall">
							<trim.para>Axle weighs 200 lbs. (91 kg). Assistance is required to lift axle. Failure to comply may result in personnel death or injury, or equipment damage. Seek medical attention in event of injury.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>The frame and axle must be firmly supported on a hard, level surface. Direct all personnel to stay clear of semitrailer when supported in air. Failure to comply may result in personnel death or injury, or equipment damage. Seek medical attention in event of injury.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>The axle must be firmly supported with suitable support device before performing steps 2, 3, and 4. Failure to comply may result in personnel injury or equipment damage. Seek medical attention in event of injury.</trim.para>
						</warning>
						<para>Align over slung axle assembly with TDRT coupling screws by measuring distance from coupling screw to centerline of spindles on front axle. Dimensions A and B (Figure 1) must be equal within 0.13 in. (3.30 mm</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Tighten U-bolts and end clamp bolts to specification on that axle only.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Align rear axle with front axle. Dimensions C and D <callout assocfig="M00074-9-2320-451-F0001"> must be equal within 0.06 in. (1.52 mm).</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Tighten U-bolts and end clamp bolts on rear axle.</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Check dimension E <callout assocfig="M00074-9-2320-451-F0001">, the lateral centerline relationship of TDRT body and axles. Dimension E <callout assocfig="M00074-9-2320-451-F0001"> must not exceed 0.25 in. (6.35 mm).</para>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<para>Recheck alignment of front axle with coupling screws and rear axle with front axle until all are aligned.</para>
				</step1>
				<figure id="M00074-9-2320-451-F0001">
					<title>Axle Alignment</title>
					<graphic boardno="G06232000402"></graphic>
				</figure>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<caution>
							<trim.para>Check all fasteners (U-bolts, end cap, trunnion hub, etc.) to ensure they are tightened to specification. Torque for all fasteners should be checked after an initial break-in period and periodically thereafter. Failure to comply may result in equipment damage.</trim.para>
						</caution>
						<para>Tighten U-bolts and nuts to specification.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
				<step1>
					<specpara>
						<warning>
							<icon-set boardno="Eye_Protection">
							<icon-set boardno="Vapor">
							<icon-set boardno="Heavy_Parts-above">
							<trim.para>Wear welding mask, gloves, and protective apron when welding or using cutting torch. Ensure eye protection is the proper tint for the welding task being performed. Failure to comply may result in personnel injury. Seek medical attention in event of injury.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Adjustment plates MUST BE WELDED BEFORE operating the semitrailer. Failure to comply may result in personnel injury or equipment damage. Seek medical attention in event of injury.</trim.para>
						</warning>
						<para>Weld the unwelded adjustment plates <callout assocfig="M00074-9-2320-451-F0002"> to spring seats.</para>
					</specpara>
				</step1>
