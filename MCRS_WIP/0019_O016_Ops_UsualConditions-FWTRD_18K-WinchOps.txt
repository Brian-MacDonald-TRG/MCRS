							<trim.para>All personnel must stand clear at a distance equal to the length of cable during winching operations. A snapped cable or shifting load could strike personnel causing injury or death to personnel. The 18K (FWTRD) winch does not have an internal brake to hold back a vehicle and is not designed for recovery of vehicles in mired conditions. It is for moving vehicles on level ground. Use only the 35K winch or a prime mover mounted 45K winch for recovery operations. Failure to comply may result in death or injury to personnel.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Winch cable can become frayed or contain broken wires. Wear heavy leather-palmed work gloves when handling the winch cable. Frayed or broken wire can injure hands. Never let a winch cable slide through hands, even when wearing gloves. A broken wire could cut through glove and cut hand.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Refer to FM 5-125 or FM 4-30.31 for inspection of winch cables. Failure to comply can result in injury or death to personnel.</trim.para>
							<trim.para>Wheels on the towed vehicle should be chocked and parking brakes engaged prior to rigging winch cables. Failure to comply could result in the vehicle moving unexpectedly creating a crush hazard causing serious injury or death to personnel.</trim.para>
